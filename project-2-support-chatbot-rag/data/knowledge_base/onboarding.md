# AcmeCRM — Onboarding Guide

## Week 1: Foundation

- Create your workspace and invite your team.
- Import existing contacts and deals from a spreadsheet or your previous CRM
  (see Data Import & Export).
- Define your sales pipeline stages to match your actual sales process —
  most teams use 5-7 stages (e.g. New, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won/Lost).
- Set up user roles: Admins manage settings and billing, Managers see team-wide
  reports, Reps see their own deals and contacts by default.

## Week 2: Connect your tools

- Connect your email provider for automatic activity logging.
- Connect your calendar so meetings show up on contact and deal timelines.
- Set up at least one Slack notification so the team sees deal updates in
  real time.

## Week 3: Automate

- Build your first automation workflow — common starting points are
  auto-assigning new leads round-robin, or sending a Slack alert when a deal
  reaches the Negotiation stage.
- Set up custom fields for anything specific to your business that isn't a
  default field (e.g. "Contract Length" or "Referral Source").

## Week 4: Report and refine

- Schedule a weekly pipeline report to go to your leadership team.
- Review the Sales Velocity report to identify which pipeline stage deals
  get stuck in most often.
- Invite any remaining team members and assign them a role.

## Dedicated onboarding support

Enterprise customers are assigned a dedicated onboarding specialist for the
first 30 days. Growth and Starter customers can book a 30-minute onboarding
call from Settings → Help → Book Onboarding Call.
