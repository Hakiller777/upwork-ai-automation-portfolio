# AcmeCRM — Billing & Plans

## Plans

| Plan       | Price (per user/month) | Users     | Key limits                         |
|------------|-------------------------|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| Starter    | $12                     | Up to 5   | 1 pipeline, 1,000 contacts          |
| Growth     | $29                     | Up to 25  | Unlimited pipelines, 50,000 contacts|
| Enterprise | Custom                  | Unlimited | SSO, audit logs, dedicated support  |

## How billing works

Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually (annual billing gives a 20%
discount). Adding a user mid-cycle prorates the charge for the remaining
days of that billing period. Removing a user takes effect immediately but
does not generate a refund for the current cycle.

## Payment methods

We accept all major credit cards and, on Enterprise plans, ACH bank
transfer or invoicing with NET-30 terms. Update your payment method any
time from Settings → Billing → Payment Method.

## Invoices and receipts

Every invoice is available as a PDF under Settings → Billing → Invoice
History. Invoices are also emailed automatically to the account owner and
any billing contacts added under Settings → Billing → Billing Contacts.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, we retry automatically after 3 days and again after 7
days. Accounts with a payment failing for more than 14 days are downgraded
to a read-only state until billing is resolved — no data is deleted.

## Upgrading or downgrading

You can change plans at any time from Settings → Billing → Change Plan.
Upgrades apply immediately with a prorated charge; downgrades apply at the
start of the next billing cycle to avoid losing access to features you've
already paid for.
