# AcmeCRM — Product Guide

## Core modules

**Contacts** — Every person or company you do business with lives in the
Contacts module. Each contact record stores basic info, custom fields,
linked deals, email history, and a timeline of every interaction.

**Deals** — Deals represent sales opportunities moving through your pipeline.
Each deal has a stage, value, close date, and owner. Drag and drop deals
between stages on the Kanban board, or update them from the deal detail page.

**Pipelines** — You can create multiple pipelines for different sales
processes (e.g. New Business vs. Renewals). Each pipeline has its own
customizable stages.

**Activities** — Calls, meetings, tasks, and notes are logged as activities
and attached to contacts or deals, giving your team a full interaction
history.

**Reports** — Pre-built and custom reports show pipeline value, win rate,
sales velocity, and rep performance. Reports can be scheduled to email
stakeholders on a recurring basis.

## Getting started

1. Import your existing contacts (CSV or from a previous CRM).
2. Create your first pipeline and define its stages.
3. Invite your team and assign roles (Admin, Manager, Rep).
4. Connect your email account for automatic activity logging.
5. Set up at least one automation workflow (e.g. auto-assign new leads).

## Navigation

The left sidebar gives access to Contacts, Deals, Reports, and Automations.
The top bar has global search, notifications, and account settings. Every
list view supports filtering, sorting, and saved views.
