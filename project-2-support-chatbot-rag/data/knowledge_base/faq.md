# AcmeCRM — Frequently Asked Questions

**What is AcmeCRM?**
AcmeCRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform for small
and mid-sized B2B sales teams. It combines contact management, deal
pipelines, email sync, and reporting in a single workspace.

**How many users can I add to my account?**
The Starter plan supports up to 5 users, the Growth plan supports up to 25
users, and the Enterprise plan supports unlimited users. You can add or
remove seats at any time from Settings → Team Members.

**Can I use AcmeCRM on my phone?**
Yes. AcmeCRM has native iOS and Android apps that sync in real time with the
web app. See the Mobile App guide for setup instructions.

**Is there a free trial?**
Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial of the Growth plan. No
credit card is required to start the trial. You can downgrade to the free
Starter plan at any time after the trial ends.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at
the end of the current billing cycle, and you keep access until then. Your
data is retained for 30 days after cancellation in case you want to reactivate.

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. All plans support CSV export of contacts, deals, and activity history
from Settings → Data → Export. Enterprise plans additionally support
scheduled automatic exports to an S3 bucket.

**Who can I contact for help?**
Use the in-app chat widget, email support@acmecrm.example, or check the
Troubleshooting guide for common issues. See the SLA and Support Tiers
document for response time guarantees by plan.
