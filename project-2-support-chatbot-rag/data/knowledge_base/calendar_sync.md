# AcmeCRM — Calendar Sync

## Supported calendars

Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook Calendar sync in both directions with
AcmeCRM.

## What syncing enables

- Meetings scheduled in AcmeCRM appear on your connected calendar, and vice
  versa.
- Meetings with a contact automatically show up on that contact's timeline.
- Your availability is visible when teammates try to schedule a meeting
  with a shared contact, avoiding double-booking.

## Connecting your calendar

Go to Settings → Calendar Sync → Connect and authorize via OAuth. You can
choose which calendar to sync if you have multiple (e.g. personal vs. work
calendar in the same Google account).

## Scheduling a meeting from a deal or contact

Click "Schedule Meeting" on any deal or contact page, pick a time (AcmeCRM
shows your connected calendar's free/busy status), and an invite is sent to
the contact automatically. The meeting is logged as an activity once
scheduled — you don't need to wait until after the meeting happens.

## Meeting reminders

AcmeCRM sends a reminder notification 15 minutes before any synced meeting,
in addition to whatever reminder your calendar app itself sends.

## Disconnecting

Disconnecting calendar sync from Settings → Calendar Sync stops future
syncing but does not delete meetings that were already logged to contact or
deal timelines.

## Troubleshooting

If new meetings aren't appearing, check that the correct calendar is
selected under Settings → Calendar Sync → Active Calendar, and confirm the
OAuth connection hasn't expired (a red "Reconnect" button appears if it
has).
